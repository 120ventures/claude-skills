# Terracotta Precision

## Design Philosophy for Analyst Reports

Terracotta Precision is the visual language of analytical clarity expressed through warmth. Rigor and warmth are not opposites — the most trustworthy communication comes wrapped in materials that feel human.

### Space & Form
Margins are wide — structural, not decorative. Sections separated by breath, not heavy rules. Dividers: single hairline in accent color. The page feels like a well-organized desk.

### Color Hierarchy
1. Warm off-white ground (never pure white)
2. Near-black for primary text
3. Accent color sparingly: section numbers, KPIs, chart bars, accent lines
4. Muted warm gray for secondary text

### Typography
- Serif (e.g. Cormorant Garamond) for headings — elegant, editorial
- Sans-serif (e.g. Inter) for body/data — precise, legible
- Font sizes step down in clear intervals. Nothing shouts.

### Data Presentation
- Tables: thin hairlines, no alternating row fills, right-aligned numbers
- KPIs: large serif + accent color
- Funnel: horizontal bars with drop-off indicators
- Callout boxes: clay background + accent left bar
- Bar charts: accent color only, minimal labels

### Craft
Every spacing decision, alignment, and color choice reflects deliberation. The craft is in the restraint.
